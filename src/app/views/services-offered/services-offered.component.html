Once at your home, Pete will inspect the property. He'll check the following:

  • Ground level - holes and cracks in the home. Under decks and sheds.
  • Mid level - dryer vents and cabling holes.
  • Top level - soffets and attic vents.

After an inspection, set up and removal begins.

  • Trapping most animals is the best way of removal.
  • Exclusion may be a viable option. This will allow animals to exit the home but not re-enter.

Upon removal completion, patch and/or barrier work is highly recommended.

  • Patch and seal all entry holes for animals.
  • Possibly repair damage caused by creation of entry points.
